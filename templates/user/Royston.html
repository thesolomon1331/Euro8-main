Royston

Royston has a fascinating history dating back to the Roman period when it was a significant settlement known as Sorviodunum. The town's name is believed to originate from "Royse's Town," referring to a local landowner. Throughout the medieval and Tudor periods, Royston thrived as a market town due to its strategic location on the crossroads of important trade routes.
